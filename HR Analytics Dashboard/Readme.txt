HR Analytics Dashboard
📊 Overview
The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to provide deep insights into key Human Resource metrics. It facilitates data-driven decision-making by visualizing trends and patterns across various HR parameters.

✅ Features
Attrition Analysis: Visual breakdown of employee attrition by age, department, education, and income level.
Demographic Insights: Track average employee age, salary distribution, and gender ratio.
Role-Specific Metrics: Insights into job roles with the highest attrition and salary variations.
Interactive Filters: Dynamic slicers for department, job role, education, and more to drill down into specific insights.

🔍 Objectives
Identify trends influencing employee turnover.
Provide actionable insights to HR teams for reducing attrition.
Enable data-backed workforce planning and strategy formulation.

🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI: For data modeling, transformation, and visualization.
Excel: Data Filteration
